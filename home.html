About Toolshop

Toolshop is South Africa’s trusted supplier of quality tools, building materials, and hardware essentials. Since our founding in 2010, we've served contractors, DIY enthusiasts, and businesses with reliable products—from hammers and drills to cement, tiles, and paint.

Our mission is to make construction and renovation easier, more affordable, and more accessible. With a growing catalog of locally sourced and international brands, we’re proud to support projects of all sizes across the country.

Whether you're building a home, upgrading a workspace, or stocking your tool shed, Toolshop is your one-stop destination for durable, affordable, and expertly selected supplies.
